Place Anything into Any Video

Ziling Liu1,2, Jinyu Yang1, Mingqi Gao1, Feng Zheng1,2,
1tapall.ai 2Southern University of Science and Technology,
Video

Abstract

The reuse and recycling of videos have demonstrated remarkable potential across a wide range of applications, which is particularly important in scenarios where capturing real-world videos is either unfeasible or costly. This paper presents a novel and efficient system, named PAAV, to place anything into any video automatically with only a picture or text description of the target object or element. It consists of three modules: 3d generation module, intrinsic estimation, video reconstruction module, 3d object insertion module. Through the user study, our system can easily and seamlessly place anything into any video with only a photo of the object.
